Slim Image
Trim the fat from your JPGs, GIFs, PNGs and SVGs. Simply Drag & Drop.

Download for macOS
Works with macOS 10.12 | 100MB

Simply drag and drop your images into Slim Image and it will trim the fat. In PNGs you can see savings of up to 90% which could significantly reduce load time for your website and apps.

Currently Slim Image is only available for macOS but a Windows version is coming.
